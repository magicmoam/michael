APRIL 10, 2026

🚧 The Gibraltar Border Is OPENING! 🚧

A comedy sketch by El Vrada — in which absolutely nobody is happy about good news

🛋️ The Living Room — Morning

El Vrada is sitting in his armchair reading the Gibraltar Chronicle. Sarah is scrolling her phone. The TV is on but nobody is watching it. A small Union Jack hangs slightly crooked on the wall.
NEWS ANCHOR (on TV):
"...and after years of negotiations, the UK and EU have finally reached a deal. The Gibraltar–Spain border will be fully open from next month, with free movement of people and goods—"
El Vrada slowly lowers his newspaper. His left eye twitches.
EL VRADA:
No.
SARAH:
What do you mean "no"?
EL VRADA:
I mean NO. No no no. Absolutely not. I didn't vote for this.
SARAH:
You didn't vote at all. You said the polling station was "too far" — it was on the same street.
EL VRADA:
It was uphill, Sarah. Uphill.

🚗 The Queue at the Border — Later

El Vrada and Sarah are in the car at the border. There is no queue. The barrier is up. Spanish guards are waving people through with big smiles.
EL VRADA:
(suspiciously) ...Why is it moving so fast?
SARAH:
Because the border is open now. That was the whole point.
EL VRADA:
But where's the queue? WHERE'S THE SUFFERING?
SARAH:
Do you... want to be stuck in traffic for two hours?
EL VRADA:
I want ORDER, Sarah. I want a man in a booth looking at my passport like I might be an international smuggler. I want him to squint at my photo and back at me three times. That's RESPECT.
📝 El Vrada's passport photo is from 2003. He had sideburns and a soul patch. He is unrecognisable.

🇪🇸 La Línea — 5 Minutes Later

They've crossed into Spain. El Vrada looks deeply uncomfortable. Everything is fine.
EL VRADA:
This doesn't feel right. We just... drove through. No stamps. No drama. Nothing.
SARAH:
It's called progress.
EL VRADA:
It's called ANARCHY.
SARAH:
We literally just passed a police checkpoint.
EL VRADA:
He waved at me, Sarah. He waved. Since when do border officials WAVE? What's next — a hug? A kiss on both cheeks? We're not in bloody France.
A Spanish border guard walks over with a friendly smile.
SPANISH GUARD:
¡Buenos días! Welcome. No need to stop anymore — just drive straight through. Enjoy Spain!
EL VRADA:
(whispering to Sarah) He's too happy. Something's going on. This is a trap.
SARAH:
It's not a trap, it's a treaty.
EL VRADA:
Same thing.

🛒 Mercadona — Shopping Trip

They are in Mercadona. El Vrada is holding Spanish olive oil like it's a live grenade.
EL VRADA:
Two euros. TWO EUROS for a litre of olive oil. Do you know what Morrison's charges? SEVEN QUID.
SARAH:
So this is good, then?
EL VRADA:
NO! Because now EVERYONE will come here. It'll be rammed. Full of people from the Rock buying cheap chorizo and boxed wine. They'll ruin it. Just like they ruined Morrisons.
SARAH:
You ruined Morrisons. You knocked over the olive display and blamed it on a child.
EL VRADA:
That child was in my way.

🐒 The Apes' Den — Back on the Rock

They're driving back up. El Vrada spots a Barbary macaque sitting on a wall near the border.
EL VRADA:
(pointing) SEE? SEE THAT?
SARAH:
It's a monkey.
EL VRADA:
It's a monkey near the border. It's thinking about leaving. Open borders, Sarah — the monkeys will defect to Spain. And once they're gone? Gibraltar is FINISHED.
SARAH:
The monkeys aren't going to—
EL VRADA:
They'll set up in Algeciras. Better weather, cheaper bananas. It's OVER.
📝 Legend has it that if the monkeys ever leave Gibraltar, the British will too. El Vrada treats this as constitutional law.

🏠 The Living Room — That Evening

El Vrada is writing a strongly worded letter. He has already gone through three pens.
SARAH:
Who are you writing to now?
EL VRADA:
The Governor. The Chief Minister. The King, if I can find a stamp. Also, the monkey conservation people — they need a heads up.
SARAH:
You're warning monkey conservationists about an open border?
EL VRADA:
Someone has to, Sarah. Someone. Has. To.
He licks an envelope aggressively. Pause.
EL VRADA:
(quietly) ...The olive oil was good, though.
SARAH:
I know.
EL VRADA:
Don't tell anyone I said that.
🇬🇮 END 🇬🇮
